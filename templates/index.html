About us

This is a simple web app created by four Deliveroo apprentices and it is our final project at Makers. Our mission was to develop a web app where food can be ordered by individuals as part of a group order. The order initiator can "Create Order" that selects the restaurant and generates a unique order token shared with whomever they want to be part of the group order.

Key ingredients for our success as a team are sharing ideas and knowledge, working together in pairs and offering help to each other whenever required.
